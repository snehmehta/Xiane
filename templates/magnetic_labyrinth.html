DESCRIPTION

Magnetic Labyrinth is a logic maze event that requires you to use Magnet and ball with concentration focus and precision in tilting controlling the ball.

Rounds: 3 rounds

1st round: In this round there is no time limit & who complete task in minimum time will qualify for second round.
2nd round: This round is time limit round and who will be completed it in minimum time will qualify for next round.
3rd round: This round has no time limit but try complete task in minimum time.
