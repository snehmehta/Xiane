DESCRIPTION

Laser Maze is a logic maze event that requires you to use mirrors, a little science and brainpower to direct the laser through a series of mind-challenging mazes and light up the target.

Rules and Regulations

  • Student must have to participant in the group of 3 students only.
  • Participants have to reach the destination by reflecting laser through mirrors covering maximum distance and points in minimum time through all the mandatory checkpoints
  • 1 Inch = 1 Point
  • Time Limit = 60 Seconds
  • Compulsory four mirror have to be used.
